Mike Flaherty

In a general sense, this series is about the topic of social activism in clay. I strongly believe that art and craft are perfectly suited for critical analysis of social and political structures and that the most successful art approaches these topics in a direct and legible manner. In a more specific sense, these pieces are about my ideas surrounding personality, culture, determinism and fatalism.

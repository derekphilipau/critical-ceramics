melissa dawn posted 27-04-2003 07:08 PM ET (US)            
hey, chris! You surely gave the"bound to earth effect" here with your work...in fact, the clay still seems to have that soft-earth feel, but one is NOT invited to touch! :) I would have like to have been able to blow up the images so that i could see the entirety of the pieces...oh, well, eh? I couldn't make out what was going on on the forehead of the second image, but i loved the detail of the fruity-green tomatoesque shapes on top. It collided with the whole form in a way that was conducive to your victim/profiteer philosophy. Keep it up, and maybe a little more clarity and precision in the future with your colors. I enjoyed viewing your work, and i love dan's primitive face! Thanks.

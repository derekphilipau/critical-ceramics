ABOUT CRITICAL CERAMICS

Dear Reader,

We've published words of critical inquiry for ceramic artists for over 5 years, now. Critical Ceramics is proud to have published unique and insightful works that would not have had an opportunity elsewhere. I invite you to explore Critical Ceramics today and to keep coming back to see where we're going.

The Critical Ceramics web site receives over three hundred visitors every day from countries ranging from Afghanistan to Yugoslavia. Although North American readers are the majority, non-U.S. readers account for over 20% of all visitors. Areas on Critical Ceramics include articles (ARTicles), reviews (re:VIEWS), current events (seen), discussions (Hear/Say), and online critiques (CRITS).

We cannot do this alone! Writers, artists and critics are needed. Articles exploring unique views, new topics, different cultures, and contemporary artists are of particular interest. We strongly encourage submissions of reviews of notable ceramic exhibitions from around the globe. Additional information on writing and reviewing for Critical Ceramics can be found on our submissions page. Accepted authors are given the opportunity to display images and a statement on our writers page.

Critical Ceramics is always looking for contributions or sponsors to help further our mission. All contributions are tax deductible. You can find more information about sponsorships here; feel free to email us at any time.

Whatever your interest, your input is critical.

Sincerely,

Forrest Snyder, Editor
